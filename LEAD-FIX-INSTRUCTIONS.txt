GOOGLE SHEETS LEAD FIX

1. Open your Google Sheet.
2. Go to Extensions > Apps Script.
3. Replace everything in Code.gs with the supplied Code.gs file.
4. Save.
5. Deploy > Manage deployments > Edit.
6. Choose New version.
7. Execute as: Me.
8. Who has access: Anyone.
9. Deploy.
